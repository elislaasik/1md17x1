There are many ways invented to make our work more productive, here are some brilliant apps, which might make your workday more productive.

Evernote

One of the best apps to store thoughts, ideas. It can be used very well as a braindump. Notes can be added in many forms: text, video, audio, sketch, web clippings. Digital notepad on your phone.

Coggle

This web app is perfect for creating mind maps. All mind maps can be shared, downloaded and different tools can be used to make all maps visibly appealing as possible.

Trello

Tool to stay organized at work and home. Whether managing a team, making a grocery list or creating a to do list. Has the posiibility to add members to work on group projects and to keep private projects separate.

Wunderlist

App to make to-do lists to get everything done. One of the best to do list apps there is. Lists can easily be made and managed for maximum productivity.

Duco

A perfect in-app guide to running design and brainstorm sprints. This makes use of the team members ideas the most and the result is normally better than hoped for.

Scanbot

The best scanner app. For scanning documents, recipes and much more. After one fast scan, cropping and the adjustment of colours, images and PDF’s can be saved in one tap.

Dropbox

Store all your files and backups in one safe place. All the files can be shared with a team and it makes collaborating simple. Dropbox community makes using and sharing the files accessible to everyone.

Timedoctor

Powerful software for tracking time to increase productivity. Meant for both individual and team time tracking. For future planning, it helps to know how long it takes to complete a task.

Forest

An app that doesn’t let you use your phone to be productive. This app helps to beat phone addiction by growing a tree each time when you don’t use your phone.
